Tool BarÂ¶

Underneath the menu bar lies the speed button bar or tool bar. This contains a series of buttons distinguished by icons. Starting from the left-hand side, the first button is the ‘New patient’ button , which corresponds to the ‘File’, ‘New’ menu option. Directly next to it is the ‘Open patient’ button , corresponding to the ‘File’, ‘Open’ menu option. The third button in this group is the ‘Save patient’ button , corresponding to the ‘File’, ‘Save’ menu option. The ‘Calculate Plan’ button is separate from these. This button calculates the treatment plan once all the data has been entered. The last speed menu button is the ‘Exit’ button . Click on this button to exit from the program. It has the same function as choosing ‘File’, ‘Exit’ or clicking on the ‘Close’ button right at the top right hand corner of the window. To use a speed menu button, simply click on it. It provides a fast and easy way of accessing the menu options.

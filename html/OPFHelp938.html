Field SizeÂ¶

‘Field size’ is a compulsory numeric field that must be entered. The field size must be between 3 cm and 40 cm. Any value outside this will result in an error dialogue box appearing, and you will not be allowed to continue until you have entered in a correct value. Enter in the Y or length value, and then the breadth or X value.

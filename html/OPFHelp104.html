Calculating/Displaying a planÂ¶

Once you have entered the data in all the fields you are now ready to calculate a plan. You can cause the Results window to be displayed either by clicking on the ‘Calculate’ button on the speed bar, or by clicking on the ‘Calculate’ option on the menu bar. This window is a scrollable page with the various parameters you have entered and the results of the treatment plan.
